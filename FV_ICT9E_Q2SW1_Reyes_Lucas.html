Star Wars: My Favorite Characters in the Prequels and Original Trilogy

Background Characters My Favorite Lightsaber Used by Them

Obi-wan Kenobi
Obi-wan Kenobi, though his origins are unknown
(although stated in the Wiki, it was only made up on the spot by George Lucas), started as a young padawan training under Qui-Gon Jinn.
After the death of his master, the Jedi council allowed him to train Anakin Skywalker, also known as the Chosen one. A few years later, the Clone Wars happened. Obi-Wan and Anakin fought together. Not long though, after Order 66, they fought each other after Anakin fell into the dark side. Obi-Wan was victorious.

Anakin Skywalker
Anakin Skywalker was a young boy in Tatooine.
After getting tested by Qui-Gon Jinn, he went with him to the Jedi Council.
He became a padawan, and started to be in love with Padme.
After the death of Qui-Gon Jinn, the Jedi council allowed Obi-Wan to train him.A few years later, after getting married to Padme, the Clone Wars happened. Obi-Wan and Anakin fought together. Not long though, after Order 66, they fought each other after Anakin fell into the dark side.
He fell into the dark side because he wanted to protect Padme.
Thinking Palpatine was an ally, he asked for his help. Unfortunately for him, he was a secret Sith-lord. After the battle, Obi-Wan was victorious, and Anakin later became Darth Vader.

Darth Maul
Darth Maul was one of Darth Sidious' apprentices.
He was born on Dathomir, and as a child, was trained by Darth Sidious.
In the Phantom Menace, he killed Qui-Gon Jinn.
After the death of Qui-Gon Jinn, Obi-wan, his apprentice, defeated Darth Maul. Though not shown in the Prequel movies, Darth Maul survived and he now has cybernetic legs.

Luke Skywalker
Luke Skywalker is the son of Anakin and Padme.
He lived most of his early life with his Uncle and Aunt on Tatooine.
He was just a moisture farmer in Tatooine.
After meeting Ben Kenobi and a few friends, he goes to the Death Star.
With the help of his friends, he destroys the Death Star.
After destroying the Death Star, he continued his Jedi training with Yoda. After training with Yoda, he fights Darth Vader, his father. Vader cuts off his arm and is victorious. However, Luke comes back for round 2 and defeats his father. Unfortunately, Palpatine attacked Luke with his Force lightning. As a last ditch effort, Darth Vader overthrows Palpatine, fulfilling the prophecy.
